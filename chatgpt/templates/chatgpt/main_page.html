ChatGPT refers to a chatbot platform that uses the GPT (Generative Pre-trained Transformer) language model to chat with humans through text-based conversations. The GPT model is a deep learning algorithm that is trained on large amounts of data, allowing it to generate human-like responses based on the context of the conversation. ChatGPT bots can be used for a variety of purposes, including customer service, personal assistance, and language learning.
